Neckklava – Innovative Neck Protection for Hockey Players

My team developed the Neckklava, a balaclava-inspired neck guard designed to improve player safety without sacrificing comfort or mobility. As part of Rethink The Rink 2024, we ideated, prototyped, and presented to experts from the Pittsburgh Penguins, Covestro, Bauer Hockey, and CMU’s College of Engineering—all in one week. Our design focused on ergonomics, advanced materials, and player usability.

Role: CAD modeling, materials research, prototyping & testing

Tacti‑Chair – Haptic Trail Guidance Seat

A tactile feedback chair concept to assist visually impaired hikers with wayfinding cues and posture guidance. We explored actuator placement, power budgeting, and durable outdoor materials for real-world trails.

Role: Mechanism design, enclosure CAD, quick-turn prototyping

Taco Bell Combat Robot – Compact Mechanical Design

A battlebot‑style robot focused on robust drivetrains, serviceable armor, and quick‑swap weapon modules. The project emphasized weight‑conscious design and rapid repairs between matches.

Role: Chassis CAD, drivetrain selection, manufacturing

Python Game & App Projects

A collection of small Python projects including a Sudoku solver/game and a vector drawing app. Focus areas included clean UI, algorithm correctness, and save/export features.

Role: Full‑stack Python, UI, and algorithm design
